Our services

Pre-construction Design and Estimating

Suprius Projects excels in providing cost-effective pre-construction services to our clients. In doing so, we are able to provide many types of pre-construction estimates, budget estimates, firm estimate, not-to-exceed price and etc. We also provide planning the design, site selection & feasibility and determine any option for value engineering.

Residential Building

We provide a full range of cost-efficient and technically sound residential building services. building service include Excavation, R.C.C work, All type masonary work, Plaster work, Painting & plumbing work. Along with this we deals in Energy distribution and supply, Facade work, Fire safety & protection, Heating ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC), Water drainage and plumbing.

MEP

Our engineers with diverse skills and extensive domain expertise create MEPF BIM models and validate these models for constructability, performance, and maintenance of the building. Our familiarity with various codes such as IPC, ASHRAE, NFPA and the ability to follow Client/industry specific Drafting standards ensures flexible solutions for the customers and assures efficient deliveries.

Industrial Construction

We have successfully delivered Industrial and Turnkey project to the client. Our clients appreciate our professionalism and project optimisation techniques. Also, we use the latest project management tools for resource optimisation, schedule tracking, budget tracking, etc to ensure smooth operations during various project phases.

Specialized Infrastructure

Being a customer focused organization we are engaged in offering Specialized Infrastructure Services. It comes with wide range of industrial and commercial projects like trimix road, VDF flooring, machine foundation, STP & ETP plant (from design to execution) and lot more.

Rainwater Harvesting

We provide a full suite of services for rainwater harvesting systems, depending on your exact need or phase of development. It includes Design, Consulting, Residential & Industrial installation, Service & maintenance, Inspection & testing etc.
